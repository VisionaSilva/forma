# Dusk — Taste Notes

## Brand Voice
Moody. Confident. Premium without arrogance. Like walking into a well-designed cocktail bar — dim lighting, everything intentional, nothing screaming for attention.

## Rationale
- Purple accent on near-black surfaces creates depth without overwhelming
- High contrast text (0.95 lightness on 0.10 surface) ensures readability
- The glow shadow on CTAs gives a subtle "lit from within" quality
- Surface elevation uses chroma increase, not just lightness — surfaces feel colored, not just layered gray

## Forbidden Moves
- ❌ No neon or saturated colors outside the accent
- ❌ No white backgrounds anywhere — even overlays stay dark
- ❌ No gradient backgrounds on sections — keep surfaces flat
- ❌ No light text on light surfaces — always check contrast
- ❌ No blue links — accent purple serves as the interactive color

## When to Use Dusk
Tech products. Crypto. Creative agencies. Anything that wants to feel premium and modern without the "corporate dark mode" energy.
