# Graphite — Taste Notes

## Brand Voice
Quiet confidence. Brutalist restraint. The design equivalent of a well-tailored black suit — no embellishments, pure structure.

## Rationale
- Near-zero chroma across the board — this is intentionally desaturated
- White-on-dark CTA with no color accent is a deliberate choice: the content IS the design
- Minimal shadow glow keeps the monochrome purity intact
- Border hierarchy uses tiny chroma shifts (0.002 → 0.005) that the eye registers subconsciously

## Forbidden Moves
- ❌ No colored accents — the absence of color IS the identity
- ❌ No playful rounded corners — keep radius tight
- ❌ No emoji or decorative elements in the UI
- ❌ No heavy shadows — lightness hierarchy replaces shadow hierarchy
- ❌ No warm tones sneaking in — hue stays locked at 270

## When to Use Graphite
Editorial. Documentation. Developer tools. Photography portfolios. Anything where the content needs to breathe and the chrome needs to disappear.
